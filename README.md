 Retail Sales Performance Analysis
 Project Overview

This project performs end-to-end retail sales analysis using Python, SQL, and Power BI. The objective is to analyze sales trends, customer behavior, and product profitability to generate actionable business insights.

 Business Problem

A retail company wants to:

Identify top-performing products and regions

Detect loss-making products

Understand customer purchasing behavior

Analyze the impact of discounts on profitability

 Tools & Technologies Used

Python (Pandas) – Data cleaning & feature engineering

MySQL – Business query analysis

Power BI – Interactive dashboard creation

DAX – Advanced measures & dynamic titles

 Project Workflow
1:Data Cleaning (Python)

Handled missing values

Removed duplicates

Converted date formats

Created business columns:

Profit Margin

Delivery Days

Sales per Unit

2️: SQL Analysis

Identified top customers

Analyzed sales by category & region

Detected loss-making products

Calculated monthly revenue trends

3️: Power BI Dashboard

Built a 3-page interactive dashboard:

Page 1 – Sales Overview

KPI cards (Sales, Profit, Orders, Margin)

Monthly sales trend

Sales by category

Sales by region

Page 2 – Customer Analysis

Top customers

Sales by segment

Orders per customer

Page 3 – Product Performance

Top 10 products

Loss-making products

Discount vs Profit analysis

 Key Insights

Technology category generated the highest revenue

Certain products were consistently loss-making

Higher discounts reduced profit margins significantly

A small group of customers contributed majority of revenue
